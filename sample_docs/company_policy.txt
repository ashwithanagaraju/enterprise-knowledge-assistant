Employee Leave and Attendance Policy

All full-time employees are entitled to 20 days of paid annual leave per calendar year.
Annual leave must be approved by the employeeâ€™s manager prior to taking time off.

Employees are eligible for up to 10 days of paid sick leave per year.
Sick leave exceeding two consecutive days requires medical documentation.

Unused annual leave cannot be carried forward to the next calendar year.
Employees are encouraged to plan their leave in advance to ensure adequate team coverage.

For questions related to leave balance, approvals, or policy clarification, employees should contact the HR department.
